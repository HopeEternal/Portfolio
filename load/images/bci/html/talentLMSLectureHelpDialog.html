Navigating Within the Lecture

Lectures are often comprised of multiple pages and can be navigated in several ways:

  1. The and buttons at the bottom of each page can be used to move forward or backward through the current lecture a page at a time.
  2. Alternatively, the icon will open a side navigation menu displaying links to all of the pages for the current Lecture.
  3. Any link can be selected from the side navigation menu to navigate directly to the desired lecture page.
